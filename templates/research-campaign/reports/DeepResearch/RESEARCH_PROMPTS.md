# Research Report Schema

Ask external research agents or humans to use this schema.

## Required Sections

1. Problem fit
2. Candidate method summary
3. Code and weights availability
4. Environment and hardware requirements
5. Integration slot or API surface
6. Expected benefit
7. Known risks
8. Minimal probe experiment
9. Promotion criterion
10. Verdict: `GO`, `NO-GO`, or `NEEDS_MORE_INFO`
